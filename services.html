Our Services

At Caliber Accounting and Business Solutions, we offer a comprehensive range of accounting and financial services designed to meet the diverse needs of businesses and individuals. Explore our services below to see how we can help you achieve your financial goals.

1. Tax Services

Our tax services are tailored to minimize your tax liabilities and ensure compliance with all applicable laws. We offer tax planning, preparation, and filing for both businesses and individuals, helping you navigate the complexities of the tax system with confidence.

2. New Business Setup with Licenses

Starting a new business? We provide expert guidance through the entire business registration process, helping you choose the right structure, file necessary paperwork, and set up your business for success from day one.

3. Sales Tax Management

Managing sales tax can be challenging, but we’re here to simplify it for you. Our sales tax management services include accurate calculation, filing, and remittance of sales taxes, ensuring your business remains compliant with state and local regulations.

4. Personal Tax Assistance

We offer personalized tax assistance to help you manage your personal tax situation effectively. From filing your tax return to providing advice on tax-saving strategies, we’re here to support your financial well-being.

5. Bookkeeping

Our bookkeeping services ensure that your financial records are accurate, organized, and up-to-date. We handle daily transactions, reconcile accounts, and provide you with timely financial reports to support your business decisions.

6. Payroll Services

Our payroll services take the hassle out of payroll processing. We handle payroll calculations, tax withholdings, and employee payments, ensuring that your team is paid accurately and on time.

7. ITIN Services

We assist individuals who need an Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN) for tax purposes. Our ITIN services include application preparation and guidance to ensure compliance with IRS requirements.
